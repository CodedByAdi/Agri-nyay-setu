Agricultural Subsidy Programs

PM-KISAN

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families.

  • Eligibility: All landholding farmer families
  • Documentation: Aadhaar, land records, bank account
  • Application: Online through PM-KISAN portal or Common Service Centers
Learn More

Crop Insurance (PMFBY)

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana provides insurance coverage for crop losses.

  • Coverage: Weather disasters, pests, diseases
  • Premium: Significantly subsidized rates
  • Claim Process: Report damage within 72 hours
Learn More

Agricultural Equipment Subsidies

Financial assistance for purchase of modern farming equipment.

  • Subsidy Rate: 25-50% of equipment cost
  • Eligible Items: Tractors, harvesters, irrigation systems
  • Application: Through District Agriculture Office
